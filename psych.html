What is Facial Recognition?

Facial recognition technology is a biometric authentication method that uses algorithms to analyze and compare an individual's facial features against a database of faces. By comparing features such as the distance between the eyes, nose, and mouth, the system can verify the identity of the person trying to access the app.

In the context of mobile payments, facial recognition offers a fast, convenient, and secure alternative to traditional payment methods such as cash, credit cards, or even fingerprints. Instead of having to remember a PIN or carry a physical card, users can simply hold up their phone to the payment terminal and let the app scan their face to complete the transaction.
